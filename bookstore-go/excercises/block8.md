# Exercise 8: Capstone — Your Real Project

**Block**: 8 — Bringing It All Together **Duration**: 30 minutes

**Goal**: Apply everything you've learned to your own real-world project.

## Tasks

1. **Setup** (5 min):
   - Open your own project (or continue with sample project)
   - Create or update CLAUDE.md with your project's conventions
   - Identify a real task: bug, feature, refactor, or documentation

2. **Apply the full workflow** (12 min):
   - **Research**: understand the relevant code with AI
   - **Plan**: create a detailed plan before coding
   - **Implement**: execute the plan with AI assistance
   - **Verify**: tests pass, code reviewed, quality checked

3. **Optimize your setup** (3 min):
   - Create at least one skill specific to your project
   - Configure one hook or MCP connection that would help your daily work
   - Document what you built so your team can adopt it

## Pair Discussion (5 min)

Compare with your partner: how did applying the full workflow to your own
project feel different from the earlier exercises? What surprised you? What
still felt awkward?

Choose **one take-away** to present to the group.

## Group Share (5 min)

Each participant presents **one take-away** from this exercise to the group.
